Coastal Clean-Up at East Coast Park

📅 Sat 25/07/2026 · 08:00–11:00 📍 East Coast Park, Area C 👥 Green Team, CommunityConnect SG

About this event

Join us for a Saturday morning shoreline clean-up along East Coast Park, Area C. Participants and volunteers work in small teams to collect and sort litter, record data for our marine waste audit, and help keep one of Singapore's favourite parks clean. Gloves, tongs and trash bags are provided — just bring a water bottle, sun protection and covered shoes. Suitable for ages 13 and up; anyone under 16 must be accompanied by an adult.

Coastal Clean-Up at East Coast Park

Organised by CommunityConnect SG — Green Team

About this event

Join us for a Saturday morning shoreline clean-up along East Coast Park, Area C. Volunteers will work in small teams to collect and sort litter, record data for our marine waste audit, and help keep one of Singapore's favourite parks clean. Gloves, tongs and trash bags are provided — just bring a water bottle, sun protection and covered shoes. Suitable for ages 13 and up; volunteers under 16 must be accompanied by an adult.
